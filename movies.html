skyCinema

skyCinema is a South African-based cinema company, and the country's largest movie exhibitor. It represents 60-65% of the market, having 55 cinema complexes consisting of 400 screens and 64,000 seats; 154 of those screens being 3D cinemas. It has several IMAX cinemas, including one in Mall of Africa, South Africa's largest shopping mall ever built in a single phase. Its head office is at the Ster-Kinekor Office Park in Sandton, Johannesburg.
